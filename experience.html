Experience

I have many years experience using Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) to treat:

  • Anxiety
  • OCD
  • Depression
  • Stress
  • Anger
  • Post traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Adjustment disorder
  • Social Phobia
  • Chronic Fatigue syndrome
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Eating disorders
  • Chronic pain
  • Bereavement
  • Long-term health problems
  • Addictions
  • Child and Adolescent problems

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy can also be used to help people with problems in many other areas of their lives, if you aren't sure please contact me and I will help you find what is best for you.
